About Enehua

Hello! My name is Enehua Ojiah, and I am a web developer with a passion for literature and storytelling.

I created this website to celebrate the works of Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie, one of my favorite authors.

Through this project, I aimed to combine my love for web design and literature while creating a resource for others to learn about Chimamanda's impactful contributions to the world.
